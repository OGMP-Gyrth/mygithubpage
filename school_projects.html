School Projects

These are some of the projects that I worked on during my education to become a software engineer.

IOS App

This app was made during an internship. The app can add, remove and edit content for a digital signage system. Made with XCode and programmed in Swift.

Android App

During a school project I was tasked with making an Android app. This app could talk to a Raspberry Pi on the RC car via a wireless network. The app included features like controlling the led's on the front, different controlling schemes and an acceleration limit.

Driving Simulator

A school project made in a group of four programmers. The goal was to make a realistic driving simulator. We used the Oculus Rift DK2, a Logitech G27 steering wheel with shifter and Unity 3D as our Game Engine.
